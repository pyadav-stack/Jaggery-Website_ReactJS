What is Jaggery?

Jaggery, also known as "Gur," is a traditional non-centrifugal cane sugar consumed in Asia and Africa. It's made by boiling raw, concentrated sugarcane juice until it solidifies. Unlike refined sugar, jaggery retains essential nutrients and is considered healthier.
